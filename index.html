Planify: Your AI-Powered Calendar Assistant

Planify revolutionizes how you create calendar events, saving you time and streamlining your scheduling process. Here's what makes Planify unique:

  • Drag and Translate: Simply drag any text from emails, messages, or documents, press Cmd+Shift+P, and watch as Planify instantly converts it into a calendar event.
  • AI-Powered Understanding: Our advanced AI interprets natural language, accurately identifying event details like time, location, and participants.
  • Cross-Platform Integration: Works seamlessly with popular calendar apps, including Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and more.
  • Context-Aware Scheduling: Planify understands context, helping you avoid conflicts and suggesting optimal times for your events.
  • Smart Reminders: Automatically sets intelligent reminders based on the event type and your preferences.
  • Privacy-First Approach: Your data stays on your device, ensuring your personal information remains secure.

Why Planify?

Planify is a revolutionary calendar extension that simplifies event creation using natural language processing. Here's what makes it special:

  • Intuitive Input: Just type your plans as you would tell a friend, and Planify does the rest.
  • Smart Recognition: Automatically identifies people, places, and times in your text.
  • Cross-Platform: Works seamlessly with Google Calendar and other popular calendar apps.
  • Time-Saving: Reduce the time spent on manual event creation and focus on what matters.
